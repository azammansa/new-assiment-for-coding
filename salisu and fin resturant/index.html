ABOUT US

Finn & Salisu is a popular fast food restaurant chain primarily based in Nigeria. It specializes in serving a variety of Intercontinent and Local dishes, including fried chicken, grilled chicken, chicken burgers, and wraps. The restaurant is known for its affordable prices, quick service, and tasty menu offerings.
